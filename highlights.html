2022 HIGHLIGHTS







Leclerc leads 1-2 for Ferrari in Bahrain GP

Charles Leclerc won a blockbuster 2022 Bahrain Grand Prix as Carlos Sainz backed up a Ferrari one-two – as Red Bull suffered a double DNF to give Lewis Hamilton the final podium place in a stunning start to the season.

Verstappen wins inaugural Miami Grand Prix over Leclerc after late Safety Car drama

Max Verstappen won the first ever Miami Grand Prix, having pried the lead off Ferrari’s pole-sitter Charles Leclerc early in the race, and defended it in an enthralling late-race battle.

Perez wins from Sainz in incident-packed wet-dry Monaco thriller

Red Bull’s Sergio Perez won his first race of 2022 by 1.1 seconds after a nailbiting end to the Monaco Grand Prix, with Carlos Sainz and Max Verstappen unable to make it past the Mexican – and Charles Leclerc losing out after dropping from pole to P4.

Verstappen leads Red Bull 1-2 as both Leclerc and Sainz retire in Azerbaijan

Max Verstappen led a Red Bull one-two in the Azerbaijan Grand Prix after retirements for Ferrari’s Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz, giving Mercedes’ George Russell P3 at Baku.

Sainz converts pole into maiden Grand Prix victory at Silverstone after scintillating race

Ferrari's Carlos Sainz has claimed his first Formula 1 victory after a breath-taking contest at the British Grand Prix, the Spaniard finishing ahead of the Red Bull of Sergio Perez and the Mercedes of Lewis Hamilton.

Heavy Silverstone Crash

A chaotic start at Silverstone saw a multi-car collision, with Zhou Guanyu barrel-rolling out of the race at Turn 1, and the race being red-flagged. The Alfa Romeo rookie was later declared fit and released from the medical centre.

Charles Leclerc Crashes Out Of The Lead

He led the race early on, but disaster struck for Charles Leclerc on Lap 18 as he crashed out of the French Grand Prix.

Verstappen Wins 2022 World Championship!

After an action-packed race in Suzuka Max Verstappen was crowned world champion for a second time.

Kevin Magnussen Achieves A MEGA Maiden Pole In Brazil!

Introducing our 106th polesitter, Kevin Magnussen!!! What a day at Interlagos for the Dane and Haas F1 Team!

Fernando Alonso And Lance Stroll Collide!

There were dramatic scenes in Austin as Fernando Alonso went airborne in a high-speed crash with Lance Stroll, but managed to continue!

Last race of the season

Max Verstappen won the 2022 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix in comfortable fashion – but Ferrari’s Charles Leclerc took P2 to finish ahead of Sergio Perez for second in the championship.
